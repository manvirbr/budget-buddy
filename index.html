How Budget Buddy Works

Starts by recording your initial balance so you always know where you began.
Lets you add income and subtract expenses anytime to keep your balance updated.
Alerts you when your balance reaches the red zone — the minimum safe balance you set.
Encourages smarter spending by reminding you when you're in the red zone before subtracting money.
Gives simple tips and insights to help you manage your money and improve financial habits.
